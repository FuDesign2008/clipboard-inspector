Frequently Asked Questions

Is my clipboard data sent to any server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your clipboard data never leaves your device.

What browsers are supported?

Modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. The Async Clipboard API requires a secure context (HTTPS).

Can I use this for debugging my own application?

Yes. The Markdown export is designed to be pasted into chat with AI assistants for debugging help.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free and open source under the MIT license.
